SmartPalate w/Practice Palate

$299.99 $399.99

Details
Empower yourself, your child, or your clients with guess-free speech training with a SmartPalate! Each SmartPalate is customized to the user's upper palate and displays real-time tongue contact during speech. SmartPalates may be purchased individually or as part of a membership.

Each SmartPalate purchase includes a FREE acrylic Practice Palate to help users acclimate to their new SmartPalate!

Upon ordering, have a local dentist or orthodontist create a “stone model” of your upper palate. You will receive a dental packet from CompleteSpeech with instructions for your dental provider as well as a pre-stamped box & shipping materials to submit the model. Your custom SmartPalate will be completed within 5 business days from the date the stone model was received.
